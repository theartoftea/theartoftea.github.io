About Us

Welcome to The Art of Tea. Didsbury's finest independent cafe bar offering fresh, locally sourced produce, loose leaf tea, along with speciality beers, wines & spirits from around the world.

While away the hours perusing the vast selection of quality second hand books available for sale in the adjoining Didsbury Village Bookshop. Free wifi for customers, artwork exhibitions from local Manchester artists and an awesome soundtrack.

Come see for yourself.

We are back - Summer 2020

We are open again! Taking it one day at a time and closely monitoring official guidance. We are continuing with our increased cleaning regime whilst also reducing the new number of tables inside and outisde the cafe.

We are offering a simplified menu and reduced hours but hope to see you soon!
